Get ready to conquer the placement and internship season and embrace the thrill of interviews! While they may seem like looming challenges, fear not. With honest guidance and targeted preparation, cracking the interview stage can become a breeze.

AASF is thrilled to announce the Mock Interview Series, to help you master the key elements and provide a taste of real industrial interview scenarios.

Imagine having the perfect platform to put yourself to the test and gain invaluable real interview experience. Well, the Mock Interview Series is precisely that platform! Additionally, we will thoroughly review your resumes and offer valuable feedback to enhance their effectiveness, ensuring that your strengths are prominently showcased.

Assessment Stages

  • There are five tracks - SDE (DSA Based), SDE (Development Based), Product Management, Data Science and UI/UX, and candidates may choose more than one track.
  • The technical round will consist of coding questions and MCQs on core subjects. This will be followed by interviews.
  • There will also be a Case Study round for students interested in managerial roles. Problem statements will be given and candidates need to detail their assumptions and strategies in a document within a deadline.
  • Personal Interview - At this stage, our respected alumni will take the mock interviews and will give you insights on how to improve your hiring chances in the real industry.

To participate in this series, register before 7th July 2024. Details regarding further rounds will be conveyed after registration.
